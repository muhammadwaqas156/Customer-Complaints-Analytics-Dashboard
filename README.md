# 📊 Customer Complaints Analytics Dashboard  

🚀 **End-to-End Power BI Project | 8M+ Records | Business Intelligence Solution**

An advanced data analytics project analyzing **8M+ customer complaints across the United States**, designed to simulate a real-world business intelligence environment.  
The dashboard transforms raw data into actionable insights for decision-making, performance tracking, and operational improvement.

---

## 🔥 Key Highlights  

- 📊 **8M+ records processed and analyzed**  
- ⚠️ Identified **~99.95% unresolved complaints**  
- 📍 High complaint regions: Florida, Texas, California  
- ⏱ Resolution delays across categories  
- 📈 Interactive executive dashboards  

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Solved  

- Which issues generate the highest number of complaints?  
- How does complaint volume evolve over time?  
- Which states contribute the most complaints?  
- How effective is the resolution process?  
- How long do complaints take to resolve?

### 1️⃣ Complaint Category Analysis

- MoM growth & rolling averages  
- Seasonality patterns  

### 3️⃣ Executive Overview  
- Total complaints KPI  
- Open vs Resolved  
- Resolution rate  
- Geographic distribution  

### 4️⃣ Geographic Analysis  
- Top 10 states  
- Complaint density map  
- **Insight:** FL, TX, CA dominate complaint volume  

### 5️⃣ Resolution Performance  
- Avg resolution time  
- Monthly trends  
- Performance tracking
